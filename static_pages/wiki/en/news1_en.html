RECORD YOUR FIRST PRET


Learning to use a new environment always takes time. To help you speed up the process and get familiar with Interprétame as soon as possible, we recorded a video tutorial that is been touring social media for a couple of weeks now. We want to share it here as well in case there are users who do not follow us on Facebook or Twitter.

You will now notice that we have embedded pop-ups like these in different sections to make navigation easier:

In addition, the video-tutorial is from now on available to you in the section Inicio>Vídeo Tutorial.
We tried to make the platform as intuitive as possible, but there are surely ways to improve it, and many of you may have suggestions or questions. We ask you to feel totally free to get in touch with us and let us hear them. You can do so through social networks, filling in our contact form or writing to interpretame.contact@gmail.com. We always try to answer right away. So far, the most common enquiry had to do with a problem with the recorder that did not let users continue to the next step. In all cases, the problem was solved by giving access to the microphone.

When you open the recorder, you should get a pop up asking you to allow the page to access your microphone. Without permission, it will not work. If the pop-up does not appear, you should be able to see a red microphone on the top bar — where the web address is — for you to give permission to the browser to access the microphone. Whatever the way, you have to make sure that the microphone is enabled to be able to record Prets and then proceed to evaluate them. We hope that all these indications make it possible for all those who have not yet recorded their first Pret to do so. And encourage those who have done so to keep on going, of course. ☺
