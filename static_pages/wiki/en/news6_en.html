Slow German to help me get started

If German is one of the challenges you are trying to face, why don't you start interpreting slowed down speeches?  Access the podcast Slow German from our search engine, find a speech about a topic you'd like to work on, and record yourself interpreting it. You can find speeches about a wide variety of topics concerning the German culture, history, music, traditions, politicians, art, and much more. A great way to start dealing with the time gap our mind needs when interpreting from German into English.

Improving my German with Harry Potter

Mixing learning with pleasure: what can be better. Have you ever considered using your favourite novels to initiate yourself in Interpreting or to enlarge your vocabulary?
If we had to choose, we'd pick Harry Potter. What about you? What audiobook would you like to start training with? Just search it in Interpretame.com and start improving right away.
